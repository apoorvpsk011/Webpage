About Sports Culture

Sports culture emphasizes teamwork, perseverance, and mutual respect. It fosters social interaction and brings people together beyond boundaries.

Merits of Sports

- Improves physical fitness
- Enhances mental well-being
- Develops leadership skills
- Encourages discipline and time management

Demerits & Challenges

- Risk of injuries
- Academic pressure for student-athletes
- Performance anxiety
- Unfair competition or doping
